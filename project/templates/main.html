Mindful Me - Your Evening Journal

Description

Mindful Me is about reflecting on your day and letting go. Track your mood, plan your tasks for the next day, and observe how your mood has evolved over the past days across different areas of your life. Identify where you feel out of balance and discover which aspects of your life could benefit from positive changes. Below you'll find a description of each category.

Daily

Reflect on your current day. How are you feeling? What was uplifting, what felt burdensome? Record your mood and thoughts to recognize patterns.

Todo

Plan your tasks for the next day. Note what you intend to do to start the next day with clarity and structure.

History

Look back at your past entries. Gain insights into your emotional development and identify situations that regularly challenge or uplift you.

Statistics

Get an overview of how your mood has changed over time in different life areas. Discover where improvements are needed and where you've already made progress.
